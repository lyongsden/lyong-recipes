Bibimbap Recipe

Description

This bibimbap recipe is simple and quick to make with fresh and prepared vegetables, an egg, and bligogi beef. The meat and egg can be taken out or replaced with something else if you're vegetarian or vegan.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Kimchi
  • Cucumber
  • Spinach
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Mushrooms
  • Radish
  • Egg
  • Bulgogi or any other meat
  • Gochujang sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the Rice
  2. Prepare the vegetables
  3. Prepare the egg
  4. Prepare the meat if there is any
  5. Mix it all up in the bowl
  6. Add the gochugang sauce
